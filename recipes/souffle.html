Souffle with chicken and penne

If you love pasta soufflés then you will definitely love this soufflé with penne, chicken, vegetables and bechamel. The ultimate food you can never get enough of!

ingredients

For the chicken

  • 600 g chicken thigh fillet
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 200 g champignon mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 tablespoon thyme, fresh, finely chopped
  • 80 g white wine
  • 250 g water

for the pasta

  • 500 g penne pasta

For the bechamel

  • 50 g seed oil
  • 50 g all-purpose flour
  • 700 g whole milk
  • 150 g reggato, grated
  • salt
  • pepper

For the composition

  • 2 eggs
  • butter, to spread on the pan
  • 30 g breadcrumbs, grated, and extra for the pan
  • 150 g reggato, grated

Steps

for the chicken

  1. Place a pan over high heat, add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and let it burn.
  2. Wearing gloves, use a cutting board to cut the chicken into very small pieces and transfer it to the pan without stirring.
  3. Saute well until nicely browned and stir once, shaking the pan.
  4. On a second cutting board, thinly slice the mushrooms, finely chop the onion and add them to the pan without stirring.
  5. Finely chop the pepper and add it to the pan without stirring.
  6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, wait 3-4 minutes, stir with a wooden spoon, and saute for 10 minutes.
  7. Add the thyme, wine and water, and simmer for 8-10 minutes.
  8. Remove the pan from the heat and transfer to a bowl.

for the pasta

  1. Place a pot over high heat, add water and salt, and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta and boil for 2 minutes less than the package instructions.
  3. Transfer the pasta with a slotted spoon to the bowl with the chicken.
  4. Mix all the ingredients well so that the pasta pulls the sauce from the chicken.

for the bechamel

  1. Place a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the seed oil and flour, and mix with a hand whisk.
  3. Gradually add the milk, making sure that the first portion is absorbed before adding the next. Pour a total of 4-5 doses.
  4. Add the 100 g of cheese in 3 batches and mix.
  5. Add salt, pepper, the pasta with the chicken and the rest of the cheese, and mix with a spoon
  6. Add the eggs and mix.

For the composition

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C degrees.
  2. Butter and sprinkle a 25x32 cm ovenproof pan with breadcrumbs.
  3. Transfer the mixture to the ovenproof dish and spread it so that it goes everywhere.
  4. Sprinkle with the cheese and breadcrumbs, transfer the pan to the oven, and bake for 25-30 minutes on the top rack.
  5. Remove the pan from the oven, set aside for 15 minutes and serve.
