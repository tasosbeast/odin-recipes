Pasticcio

Ingredients

3 tbsp olive oil

1 onion

2 carrots

2 bay leaves

1 stick cinnamon

3-4 peppercorns

1 clove garlic

1 tsp granulated sugar

1.200 g minced beef

3 tbsp tomato paste

400 g concasse tomatoes

2 chicken cubes

200 g water

1/2 bunch parsley, finely chopped

Steps

Place a pot with the olive oil on high heat.

Finely chop the onion and carrot, transfer them to the pot, and sauté well.

Add the bay leaves, the cinnamon stick, the peppercorns in a sachet, and the garlic, and stir with a wooden spoon.

Add the sugar and the minced meat, and sauté until it gets a nice color for 3-4 minutes.

Throw in the tomato paste and stir.

Add the concasse tomatoes and continue stirring.

Add the chicken cubes and the water, and cook for 10-15 minutes until the minced meat is set.

Remove the pot from the heat, take out the sachet, the bay leaves, and the cinnamon, and throw in the finely chopped parsley.

Leave the minced meat aside until we need it.
