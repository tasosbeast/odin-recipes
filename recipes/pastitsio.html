Pastitsio

Classic and traditional pastitsio but with a twist of Akis! Put feta cheese in the pasta and 2 eggs, so they don't fall apart when you cut them.

Pastitsio is a dish with origins in Italy. Its main ingredients are pasta and minced meat. It is baked in the oven and served hot as a main course.

ingredients

for the beef

  • 750 g ground beef
  • 2 onions
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh thyme
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 100 ml red wine
  • 400 g canned tomato
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 2 cubes of maggibeef
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 bunch of parsley, finely chopped

For the bechamel

  • 100 g butter
  • 100 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 litre of whole milk
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 100 g any greek hard cheese or parmesan cheese, grated
  • 3 yolks, from medium eggs

For the pasta

  • 300 g pasta for pastitsio(this is usually number 2)
  • 30 ml olive oil,
  • 1/2 bunch of parsley,
  • 1/2 bunch of thyme
  • 2 eggs, medium
  • 200 g feta cheese

Steps

for the beef

  1. Finely chop the onions, garlic and thyme.
  2. Place a saucepan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of olive oil, the onions, garlic, thyme and sugar, and sauté for 3-4 minutes, until caramelized.
  3. Add the tomato paste and sauté for 2 more minutes.
  4. Add the minced meat and break it up with a spoon.
  5. Sauté for 4-5 minutes until golden. If necessary, add 2 tablespoons more olive oil.
  6. Add the wine and allow the alcohol to evaporate completely.
  7. Add the canned tomatoes, cinnamon, cubes and bay leaves.
  8. Cook over low heat for 10-20 minutes until the food has absorbed all the liquid.
  9. Remove the pot from the heat and remove the bay leaves.
  10. Add the parsley, mix, and set aside.

for the bechamel

  1. Place a saucepan over medium heat, add the butter and let it melt.
  2. Add 100 g of flour and mix well with a hand whisk for a few minutes until it is toasted.Be careful to get the flour from the sides of the pan as well so that it doesn't burn.
  3. Add the milk in several batches, stirring constantly with a hand whisk.
  4. As soon as the first bubbles appear, remove the pot from the heat.
  5. Add the salt, pepper, nutmeg and grated cheese, and whisk to reduce the temperature.
  6. Add the yolks and mix again until the ingredients are completely combined.

for the pasta

  1. Place a pot over high heat, add water and salt and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta and boil for 2 minutes less than the package instructions.
  3. Remove the pot from the heat, drain the pasta and return it to the pot.
  4. Add the olive oil, mix and transfer the pasta to a rectangular baking pan measuring 25x35 cm.
  5. Finely chop the thyme and parsley and add them to the baking pan.
  6. Add the eggs and feta cheese and mix well with your hands.

For the pastitsio

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C in the air.
  2. Add 1 large tablespoon of bechamel to the minced meat and mix well to incorporate.
  3. Pour the minced meat over the pasta and spread it well with a spoon.
  4. Pour the bechamel on top, spread it well and sprinkle with the grated cheese.
  5. Transfer the baking pan to the oven and bake for 40 minutes or until the bechamel turns golden.
  6. Remove the baking pan from the oven and set aside to cool and firm up for 30 minutes.
  7. Cut into pieces and serve.
