Marry Me Gnocchi

This marry me gnocchi has the bacon, sun-dried tomatoes, and creamy sauce that will have you proposing to it in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound bacon
  • 1 (16 ounce) package gnocchi
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup freshly shaved Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sun dried tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Place a heavy skillet on the stove; lay bacon slices flat in the cold pan, without overlapping. Turn the stove to medium heat.
  2. After 5 to 7 minutes, the bacon should start to curl and brown. Use tongs to flip each slice over. Cook the other side until as crisp as you like, 5 to 7 minutes more. Remove bacon from the pan; place it on a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess grease. Once cool enough to handle, roughly chop bacon.
  3. Add gnocchi to the skillet. Do not stir. Cook for about 5 minutes. Add minced garlic. Saut√© until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  4. Add chicken broth and slowly pour in cream; stir to combine. Add in Parmesan cheese. Stir to melt. Stir in bacon, sun dried tomatoes, Italian seasoning, and red pepper flakes.
